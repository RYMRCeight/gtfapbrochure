🎯 Qualifications

  • Natural-born Filipino
  • Tangalan Resident (3+ years)
  • Enrolled in college within Aklan
  • No grade lower than 75%
  • Income max ₱130,000/yr
  • Voter of Tangalan (Applicant & Parent/s)
  • No other scholarships

📋 Requirements

  • Application Form with (2x2 ID)
  • Photocopy of Report Card/Grades
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character from the School
  • Voter's Certificate (Applicant & Parent/s)

A scholarship initiative of the Municipal Government of Tangalan to support low-income families.

₱4,000

📚 To Continue

  • Maintain 75% GPA or higher
  • No dropped subjects
  • Plant 5 seedlings per semester
  • Community service participation

🩸 Optional: Blood donation to MHO/PRC.

Limitation: Only one (1) member per immediate family may avail.
